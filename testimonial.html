We smile because we have got hope with us and believe that our sad stories will turn into success!

He lost his parents in an accident when they were traveling to their village in Masaka district. Timothy was too affected because he spent over three months in hospital surviving on oxygen support machines. After being treated he started staying with his relatives but he was mistreated badly with no food and denied access to shelter. We found him on street and rescued him. He joined the rest of children in our home and we started feeding him three meals a day plus other food supplements and providing him with medication.

When things where worse for everyone in the Lockdown, a single mother of three kids disappeared from home after failing to provide even a half a meal for her little ones, leaving them homeless and starving! When we heard news about that, we responded quickly and rescued her. It was so scary for her that even when we took her in to become part of our Family, it took several months for her to get over it, daily nightmares and up to now she hates darkness so much! We thank God that she is growing, attending school with good grades and is becoming an intelligent young girl who loves Jesus as she believes that it’s by grace that she lives!

Rescued from deep neglect at 9 months old, very sick and malnourished weighing just 10 pounds. His short life had been full of incredible sadness and survival, not exactly what a little baby should be dealing with. He was seen by a doctor and got several antibiotics for the 3 different infections that filled his weak body. Within a few days he started responding to the medication, nutritious food, and sweet love from us. He is now healthy, part of a family that absolutely adores him and has a future that has been re-written and is filled with hope.
