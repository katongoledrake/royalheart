Our Stories!

We smile because we have got hope with us and believe that our sad stories will turn into success!

He lost his parents in an accident when they were traveling to their village in Masaka district. Timothy was too affected because he spent over three months in hospital surviving on oxygen support machines. After being treated he started staying with his relatives but he was mistreated badly with no food and denied access to shelter. We found him on street and rescued him. He joined the rest of children in our home and we started feeding him three meals a day plus other food supplements and providing him with medication.

He grew in slum areas of Kampala with their elder siblings. Their father passed away due to alcohol. At early ages of 14 years, his siblings got married so that they would get where to get good shelter and what to eat. Morris was left with his poor mum who couldn’t take care of him. She was also an alcoholic who could even reach an extent of beating people with no reason and undressing herself in public. Morris had bad moments like poor hygiene, hunger and was a drop out from school! At the age of 8 years, his mum died and was left with no one to take care of him. Family relatives chased him from their and started sleeping on streets. One day he walked so many miles for so many days, when his feet could move no more, decided to sleep at the market where we found him! His feet were swollen, fingers were in a very bad shape and could barely hold nothing! He is a very humble boy, it took him some weeks to start talking to the rest of the kids when we got him sleeping from the market. He is calculative with his words, with a very soft heart, he fears to hurt anyone especially with his words — Loves Jesus and doing great in school with good grades. He has a dream of becoming a doctor and save more little souls. Morris urgently needs a sponsor to be able to feed, receive Healthcare, Education and many more basic needs of life!

He grew in a poor family with his father, two brothers and one sibling. He became an orphan when her mother died after giving birth. They were staying in remote areas where the family could not afford transport costs to the hospital. “The local women helped the mother in giving birth but they didn’t have more knowledge about how to handle the mother. She was already tired and bleeding too much. After like three hours, she couldn’t breathe and died.” His elder sister tells us while crying. His father was a potter who used to mold charcoal stoves for survival but could not manage to fulfill the basic needs at home and educating them. At the age of four years, with his brothers would trek three kilometers from home to the nearby trading center to sell off charcoal stoves on their heads for only UGX 2,000( 55US cents) each. To earn they had to walk an additional two kilometers to the nearest town where they would sell them off at UGX 3,500( 97US cents). Sometimes they could return back home when they haven’t sold even a single stove and the father would mistreat them for not coming home with money! He was studying in a local school but most times would miss class looking for money. One day, together with Sister Oliver, we had travelled to their village for Gospel outreach. After impacting them with the word of God, sister Oliver asked, “ who has accepted Jesus as his or her personal savior?” He came in front and said he wants us to pray for him and his family and said he wants to talk to us. We listened to him and Sister Oliver cried hearing his story. We went to the family where they staying together with their local chairman and we rescued him! Now this is the bravest little kid I have ever met, you can take a week without hearing his voice if don’t you ask him anything but, he plays a lot almost more than anyone else, there is no way you tell him that you can’t do that because it requires training and practice, he ask to start the training right away and if you don’t keep an eye on him he injure himself in the process. He is very focused at school and prays every day so that we can be able to rescue his brothers he left in village.
