Faq

Get answers to your ftrequently asked questions about our home, how we serve and more.

Namwanja Children's Home is a registered community based orphanage home striving for the betterment of vulnerable children in our community.

We strive to break the cycle of most vulnerable children’s lives by loving them as our own, helping them get access to all basic needs and putting an everlasting smile on the girl child, youths, women, orphans, the elderly and disabled in the community.

Yes, you can choose to sponsor a specific child’s education or healthcare needs, or you can support a particular project within our organization. Your sponsorship allows for a direct and personal connection with the child or project you choose to support, and you will receive updates on their progress and well-being. Please contact us for more information on sponsorship opportunities.

You can contribute to our mission in several ways. You can make a donation through our website or participate in our fundraising events. Additionally, we welcome volunteers and partnerships with organizations that share our goals. Your support, whether financial or through your time and expertise, makes a meaningful impact on the lives of children.

1. Make a Real Difference: Your fundraiser can provide essential support to vulnerable children, improving their lives and futures.
2. Support a Noble Cause: Namwanja Children's Home cares for orphaned, abandoned, and disadvantaged children, giving them a safe and loving environment.
3. Meet Basic Needs: Your fundraiser can help cover basic needs like food, shelter, education, and healthcare for the children.
4. Empower Their Future: By supporting their education and development, you can help the children build a brighter future.
5. Community Building: Fundraising for Namwanja Children's Home can bring people together, fostering a sense of community and social responsibility.
6. Transparency and Accountability: By partnering with a reputable organization like Namwanja Children's Home, you can ensure that funds are used efficiently and effectively.
7. Personal Growth: Leading a fundraiser can be a rewarding experience, teaching valuable skills and leaving a lasting impact.
Remember, every contribution counts, and your efforts can bring hope and positive change to the lives of these children!

We welcome passionate volunteers! You can join us by helping at events, spreading awareness online, or contributing your skills. Visit our website to get started.
