Children's Needs

We live in a world in which we can share responsibilities towards children. It’s a life-changing message of love and hope. Let’s spread God’s love through our actions and generosity.

Food & Nutrition

We are dedicated to nurturing the physical and emotional well-being of children in need. We provide nutritious meals to over 30 children which fuels their growth and development.Children's needs are diverse and our monthly budget helps us to attend to them and also cater for emergencies that may happen. A gift of $5, $10, $15, $20 , $40 or any size of your gift changes the course of children.

Discipleship

We believe God doesn’t want us walking this journey alone, He wants us to grow together. We believe it’s a journey of transformation and allows us to walk in our true identity as sons and daughters of God. Every child desrves to know Gos's love and purpose for their lives. we equip them with Biblical knowledge, spiritual disciplines, moral integrity and a heart of serving others.

Education

Empowering children through our Education is the best gift we can give to a child. It shapes children and what they aspire to be in future. It’s about creating a safe and loving environment where they can learn, grow and develop. Easy access to all scholastic materials and school tuition enables them to focus on their studies. Through this we are equiping children with knowledge and breaking cycles of poverty.

Community

Our commitment to community outreach extends far beyond the boundaries of our home. We firmly believe in the importance of supporting and nurturing the entire community, especially those who are vulnerable and in need. Our outreach program is dedicated to feeding the less fortunate members of our community, including fellow children, elders and widows who often find themselves in challenging circumstances.

Shelter

Every child who walks in our doors discovers a safe home where we love children as our own. Too many children grow up without the security of a loving family, our home is filled with consistent care and Christian love where children needs to heal and grow without fear.

Medical Care

Many orphaned children suffer silently from illness and malnutrition and healing is at the heart of our home. From daily hygiene education, mental health support, treatment of common childhood illness and sanitation. Promoting this culture contributes much in fostering the physical and well-being of our children and community.

Our future home.

Since we began this journey in 2021, God has faithfully provided for the children at our home. Yet as we grow, we now give full care support for 28 children. We now feel a deep calling to move beyond our current rented home into a permanent future home. A place designed with love, where more children can grow for generation to come. Give to our future home fund!
